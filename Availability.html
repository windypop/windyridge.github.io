Bookings and prices for 2021 and 2022

The cottage typically has availability ranging from £200 to £500 per week, depending on the time of year.

As bookings may come in before this web page can be updated, it's best to phone us on 01352 781615.

A deposit is kindly requested, to reserve the booking, which will be accounted for when payment is due at the start of the holiday period. Holiday checking-in time is between 3:00 pm to early evening and check-out before 11:00am.

What is included

Bed linen for the main and small bedrooms; towels; electricity for heating, lighting, shower etc. and all cottage equipment; parking for up to 3 cars; general assistance concerning the area.

What you'll need to bring

Toiletries and beach towels; buckets and spades; kites; sleeping bags (possibly); walking gear; wet weather gear (sadly we do get rain here too).

What we could help with

Travel cot and high chair; planning walks and general local advice.
